Remembering the Unknown

The Sphinx was originally created to honor the knowledge and architectural talent that the cats had before it was changed to a mythical creature with the head of a human and the body of a lion.

That’s why the position of the Sphinx resembles that of a cat. Not only because cats were worshiped, but because it was a homage to the “divine” creatures.
